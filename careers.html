Why Work With Us?

The ICT Department at Nairobi Adventist Hospital is at the forefront of integrating cutting-edge technology into healthcare. We offer a dynamic and collaborative environment where innovation is encouraged, and professional growth is prioritized. You'll work on impactful projects that directly contribute to improving patient care and operational efficiency.

We are looking for passionate, skilled, and dedicated individuals who are eager to make a difference in the intersection of technology and health. If you thrive in a challenging yet rewarding environment, explore our opportunities below.

IT Support Technician

Nairobi, Kenya

Full-time

Provide first-line technical support to hospital staff, troubleshoot hardware and software issues, and assist with system maintenance.

View Details

Responsibilities:

  • Respond to IT support requests via phone, email, and in-person.
  • Install, configure, and maintain computer hardware, software, and peripherals.
  • Assist with network connectivity issues and basic server troubleshooting.
  • Document IT procedures and solutions.

Requirements:

  • Diploma or Bachelor's degree in IT, Computer Science, or related field.
  • 1-2 years of experience in an IT support role.
  • Strong knowledge of Windows OS, MS Office, and basic networking.
  • Excellent problem-solving and communication skills.

Cyber Security Analyst

Nairobi, Kenya

Full-time

Monitor, detect, and respond to security incidents, ensuring the protection of hospital data and systems.

View Details

Responsibilities:

  • Conduct vulnerability assessments and penetration testing.
  • Implement and manage security solutions (firewalls, IDS/IPS).
  • Develop and enforce security policies and procedures.
  • Provide security awareness training to staff.

Requirements:
